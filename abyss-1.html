ABOUT THE UNIT

Born inthe pressureof the unknown

ABYSS was founded by a collective of marine biologists, engineers, and deep-sea pilots united by one obsession — the 95% of our oceans that have never been explored.

We build the vessels. We train the divers. We document what lives where no sunlight has ever reached. Every dive writes a new chapter of Earth's hidden history.

  • Autonomous ROV fleet with AI-assisted navigation
  • Bioluminescence mapping & spectral analysis
  • Real-time acoustic imaging at 11km depth
  • Cryogenic specimen preservation systems
  • Open-access scientific data publication
